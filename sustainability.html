Sustainability

Funding & Duration

The project is fully funded through collaboration with six universities in Malaysia via the Ministry of Higher Education.

The project is planned for a 12-month duration, ensuring thorough implementation and assessment.

University Impact

This initiative will help course instructors incorporate our online module into their teaching.

Students will learn to employ appropriate metadiscourse features in their professional reports.

The language constructs extracted from report samples provide practical examples of metadiscourse in action.

Community Benefits

Our innovation serves as a valuable teaching resource in writing classrooms for both academics and students.

Writers across various genres can benefit, including those focused on creative writing, academic writing, and beyond.

Society Impact

This innovation contributes to the body of knowledge on metadiscourse features in professional report writing.

It examines how metadiscourse usage affects readers' understanding of reports' concepts and content.

The project will establish guidelines for authors to include metadiscourse elements in reports, addressing issues in professional writing.

National Contribution

Our step-by-step guidelines and assessment instruments help content developers construct proper guidelines for writing courses.

The online module includes pre-tests and post-tests of Metadiscourse Awareness, along with writing samples and prompts.

This initiative ensures that professional reports become more comprehensible and reader-friendly while helping students develop professional writing skills.
