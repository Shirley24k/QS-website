According to the students' reflection essays, the individuals who incorporated the most metadiscourse features are able to guarantee that their reflection essays exhibit enhanced clarity, logical progression, and contextual relevance. Writers might employ metadiscourse to improve their communication, crafting clear messages and enabling readers to interpret those messages more thoughtfully. This will also enhance awareness of how language functions in a practical context.
